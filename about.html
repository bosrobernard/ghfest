GHANAFEST ALBERTA

A FESTIVAL OF TRADE, ARTS & CULTURE FOR PEACE & UNITY

THE STORY BEHIND

Ghanafest Alberta Festival of Trade, Arts and Culture is an annual event organized by the Ghanafest Alberta Inc and Ghanafest Alberta Foundation together with the Ghana High Commission in Canada (Ottawa) under the auspices of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Regional Integration (Ghana), Ministry of Tourism, Arts and Culture (Ghana), Ministry Of Chieftaincy And Religious Affairs, and the Ministry of Trade and Industry (Ghana) geared towards promoting Trade, our cultural diversity and ensuring peace and unity among all citizens.

A Grand Launch of the Celebration of the Festival of Arts Culture, Ghanafest Alberta 2024 in Edmonton Canada happened on the 18th of November 2023. There was a special Charity event to feed the homeless and the street that preceded the Launch in October 2023.

The Provincial Launch and Inauguration of the Organizing Committee was chaired by Ambassador Anselm Ransford Sowah, the Ghana High Commissioner to Canada, who was the Special Guest of Honor. The President of the GHANAFEST ALBERTA FOUNDATION, who doubles as the GHANAFEST ALBERTA INC CEO, Ms. Esther Nana Araba Assabil who also was the former International Ambassador of Ghanafest Toronto Canada inaugurated the Organizing Committee for the main event which was slated for July 20, 2024.

MISSION

To create an atmosphere based on dignity, inclusiveness and self-awareness that is focused on respect for diverse cultural identities. To position Ghana as a vibrant, proud, and globally relevant nation by celebrating our culture and leveraging diaspora networks for economic opportunity, trade, and youth empowerment.

VISION

To unite communities through diversity, universal language of performance, promotion of cross-cultural acceptance, and celebrating the richness of our differences. To showcase Ghana's rich heritage in Canada while building a bridge for investment, employment, entrepreneurship and cultural exchange between Ghana and the diaspora. We envisage a world where artistic expressions knows no borders, and where creative art embraces inclusivity.

THE FESTIVAL

GhanaFest Alberta Festival

GhanaFest Alberta is the flagship event of Ghanafest Alberta Inc and Foundation, an annual cultural festival held in Edmonton, celebrating Ghanaian heritage, arts, and community.
