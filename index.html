Who We Are

GhanaFest Alberta Inc and the GhanaFest Alberta Foundation are dedicated to celebrating and promoting Ghanaian culture, heritage, and community engagement in Alberta. Their overarching mission is to bridge cultures and create opportunities by showcasing Ghanaian identity and fostering global partnerships.

GhanaFest Alberta Inc.

GhanaFest Alberta Inc. aims to foster unity and appreciation for Ghanaian culture within the broader Canadian multicultural landscape. As the primary organizer of the annual GhanaFest Alberta festival, GhanaFest Alberta Inc focuses on:

Cultural Celebration

Highlighting Ghanaian traditions through music, dance, art, and cuisine.

Community Engagement

Creating inclusive events that bring together diverse communities in Alberta.

Economic Empowerement

Providing platforms for local vendors and artisans to showcase their products.
